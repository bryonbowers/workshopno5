About Us

Workshop No. 5 was founded on a commitment to craftsmanship and sustainable design. We create spaces that are both beautiful and functional, inspired by real-life needs and the use of natural materials. Our approach focuses on rethinking traditional architecture.

While we draw on modern design ideas, our work is timeless. Every project is shaped by collaboration and a clear understanding of our clients' needs, delivering spaces that truly exceed expectations.

Workshop No. 5 is dedicated to supporting women in architecture. We aspire to be a guiding light, inspiring future generations of women to break barriers, challenge norms, and pave their path to leadership in all areas of design.
